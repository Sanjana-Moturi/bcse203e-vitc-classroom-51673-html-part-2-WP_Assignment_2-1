Chocolate Cake

Home

Ingredients

Ingredients for the Cake:
  • All-purpose flour – 1 ¾ cups
  • Cocoa powder – ¾ cup
  • Sugar – 2 cups
  • Baking powder – 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Baking soda – 1 ½ teaspoons
  • Salt – 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs – 2 large
  • Milk – 1 cup
  • Vegetable oil – ½ cup
  • Vanilla extract – 2 teaspoons
  • Boiling water – 1 cup

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Add eggs, milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. Gradually mix in the boiling water until the batter is smooth.
  6. Pour the batter evenly into the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cakes cool for 10 minutes, then remove from pans to cool completely.
  9. Frost with your favorite chocolate frosting before serving.

Nutritional Facts

  • Calories: ~350-400 per slice
  • Carbohydrates: ~50g
  • Protein: ~5g
  • Fat: ~15g
